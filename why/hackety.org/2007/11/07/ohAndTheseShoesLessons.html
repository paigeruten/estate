Oh, A Blog Crammed With Shoes

November 7th 23:29
by why

So there’s a list of assorted lessons on the Shoes wiki now. And, whoa, I had totally overlooked the set of seven lessons at Juixe TechNow. Yes, believe it, there are actually at least seven things in Shoes!

These lessons really focus on the animation and drawing stuff in Shoes. I’d highly recommend the 2-D Examples post, it has the juixiest examples of the bunch. The bubbles one is a really cool use of masking.

The full set:

  • Running With Shoes
  • 2-D Graphics and Animation
  • More 2-D Examples
  • Shapely
  • Colorfy
  • Transform
  • Show Me More

Whoever put these together really plundered the Shoes source code to do this. Lots of bits of advice and little bug reports that reveal a careful combing.
