Lastly, the Sinking Symphony Video #

by why in cult

New on the Starry Afternoon Torrents, the video which aired at OSCON only—entitled The Sinking Symphony. Ignore the first minute, it was all puppetry business. This video is sort of like Bill Cosby’s Picture Pages, where they’d pan over a picture book or show a cartoon and he’d tell a story. Or Peewee or something like that.

The tracker was broken for a bit yesterday, but it’s well-seeded today. If you have no idea what I’m talking about, just pick from any of these:

The Sinking Symphony (95 MB) The Least Surprised in ‘Time.now.is_a? MagicTime’ (49 MB)
The Least Surprised in ‘The Red Scared’ (33 MB) Foxes Tall and Small on Modules (38 MB)

I just don’t want anyone to be left out of the inside jokes. We all want to hide under this trenchcoat together.

Hey, if you’re having trouble, check your BitTorrent client for errors, my tracker requires a newer version of the protocol. You may need to update.
