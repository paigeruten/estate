7 Assignments #

by why in cult

I have huge aspirations for each of you. Only I see who you really could be. Hence, I’m assigning you the following achievements. Please add them to your other 36 chores.

  1. Dispatch a dot-release via UPS ground only.
  2. Traffic duck pix within MIME headers (minimum 64k/month).
  3. Leave likeness of Matz on the Etch-a-Sketch in the dentist’s receiving room. Add under his face: matz.
  4. Color hand red. Shake with others. Leave solid print on animals.
  5. Decorate office or apartment stairwell with framed source code. (Credit the author and era on brass plaque.)
  6. Equip colleagues with secret gift of Ctrl.Alt.r, emergency RedHanded hotkey.
  7. Exude childlike bewilderment.
