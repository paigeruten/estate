Ruby-Lang Redesign Has Legs! #

by why in cult

Remember that scrappy Ruby.org mockup from a month back?? Well, things have been steadily moving on a redesign of Ruby-Lang, thanks to the persistent efforts of Curt Hibbs, Ben Giddings, and a wealth of folks from ruby-talk.

Matz gave his saintly blessings to organize a Visual Identity Team and our friends who manage Ruby-Lang have joined the team to help guide us.

As of today, Ben Giddings, John Long, Michel Martens and myself act as the core team and we will be presenting some designs in a week or two. We already have some great designs in the works and I can’t wait to slide them out under your noses!

The daily public discussion and announcements can be followed on the vit-discuss list.
