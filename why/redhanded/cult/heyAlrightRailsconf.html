Hey, Alright, RailsConf! #

by why in cult

Outrageous, my friends. The RailsConf is on. June 22nd through the 25th, 2006. In Chicago. It is going to be a great event. Many people will fall in love and have children. Some will see spirits. One man will suddenly erupt into Danish profundities.

John W. Long also sends this loverly adornment, a bracelet for the militant peacemakers who insist that Ruby and Python peoples better start getting along soon. He cites the Snakes and Rubies as the perfect summit for picketing in behalf of neutrality.

If you haven’t submitted your questions to the above event, you have a few days left to pile them up. Like: what are these two guys going to do in seven years when their language is stale bread crusts? My hairdressing business should have picked up by then, so I’ll be fine. But what about everyone else?!
