Me & the Thirsty Cups at SXSW in March, Then RailsConf in June #

by why in cult

Great sobs of clapter to Hugh Forrest, Justin Bankston and also Tom Lockney (who told them fables of FOSCON) for giving us a slender green spotlight at SXSW. Well-mannered and full-featured, each of them.

And we’re so honored to be tagging along at RailsConf. A performance of all possible facial stunts to David Double-H and Chad Singular-F for giving us a lifetime supply of rafts, plenty to ease the trip we’ll make from our home nation of the Dominican Republic. These are the things humans will do in the name of bedevilment.

But most of all: Oliver, Din, Leonie and Trudy. Those dishes and their incurable heatstroke. I love them! But, to bring this back to a more serious note, might I add: I love them.

If this is too much self-promotion, please just leave (in all caps) BLOCKADE as your message in the comments. I will respond in kind with an italicized flavor of wood finish doubling as an insult.
