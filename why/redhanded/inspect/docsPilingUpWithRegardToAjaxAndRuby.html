Docs Piling Up With Regard to Ajax and Ruby #

by why in inspect

Two excellent Javascript libs have been riding sidecar with Rails: Sam Stephenson’s Prototype and Thomas Fuch’s Script.aculo.us. These libraries have long been twiddled with in the nimble hands of scripting masters, but have lacked much documentation. However, the script.aculo.us wiki is now having a fill-in-the-blanks party for both libs.

The Prototype section looks to be about halfway. With plenty of great examples in the two areas that matter most: the Ajax.Request and Ajax.Updater objects. The Script.aculo.us section is very comprehensive, unfolds all over the place. Amazing what can happen now that Instiki has a robust backend.
