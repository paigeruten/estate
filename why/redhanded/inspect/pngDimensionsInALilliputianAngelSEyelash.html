evl: not as a one-liner though.

Here’s the equivalent in Python (which requires an extra import):

&gt;&gt;&gt; from struct import unpack
&gt;&gt;&gt; unpack('LL', open('monkey.png').read()[0x10:0x18])
(166L, 100L)

You can do it as a one liner with an ugly import hack:

&gt;&gt;&gt; __import__('struct').unpack('LL', open('monkey.png').read()[0x10:0x18])
(166L, 100L)

Nice trick!
